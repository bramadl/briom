# Briom — User Stories

## Discussion Creation

As a user, I want to create a discussion room so I can explore ideas within a dedicated shared thinking space.

As a user, I want to invite specific AI participants into a room so different perspectives can exist within the same discussion.

---

## Shared Context

As a user, I want all AI participants to share the same conversational context so I no longer need to repeat or relay information manually.

As a user, I want to introduce topics, problems, ideas, or ongoing thought processes into discussion so the room understands what is being explored.

---

## Guided Discussion

As a user, I want to decide which AI participant responds next so I can guide the direction of discussion intentionally.

As a user, I want one AI model to respond to another AI model’s reasoning so ideas can evolve through interaction.

As a user, I want to ask specific AI participants to:

- critique an idea
- expand on a point
- challenge assumptions
- offer alternative perspectives
- synthesize discussion

so the discussion becomes deeper and more useful.

---

## Conversational Flow

As a user, I want discussions to feel sequential and alive rather than simultaneous and chaotic.

As a user, I want AI participants to suggest responses or reactions without fully taking over the conversation flow.

As a user, I want to remain the moderator of the room so the discussion stays intentional and human-led.

---

## Reflection & Synthesis

As a user, I want an AI participant to summarize the discussion objectively so I can review key perspectives, agreements, disagreements, and conclusions.

As a user, I want discussions to help refine understanding rather than simply generate answers.

---

## Product Experience

As a user, I want Briom to feel like a collaborative thinking environment rather than a traditional chatbot.

As a user, I want complex thinking to feel organized, dynamic, and intellectually engaging.
